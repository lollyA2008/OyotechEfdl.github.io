A PLACE WHERE We help educators around the world use technology to solve tough problems.

We help education ecosystem in Nigeria and Africa as a whole use technology to solve tough academic challenges. EfDI brings a wide range of digital transformation solution content and tools that drive innovations across learning and teaching environment to help deliver: a better-technology driven in-classroom and virtual engagement, Coding, Gaming and Robotics curriculum for kids, Lifelong Teaching Aids for educators (Digital Age Skilling) and EdTech Advisory and training for government and educational agencies.
